Gardening Tips & Tricks

In addition to our professional services, we want to empower you with knowledge to care for your garden between visits. Here are some essential gardening tips:

  • Know Your Soil: Perform soil tests to understand nutrient levels and pH balance; amend soil accordingly.
  • Choose Native Plants: Native species are better adapted to the local climate, requiring less maintenance and water.
  • Water Wisely: Water early in the morning or late in the evening to minimize evaporation and maximize absorption.
  • Mulch Regularly: Mulching retains soil moisture, suppresses weeds, and regulates soil temperature.
  • Prune Strategically: Remove dead or diseased branches to promote healthy growth and flowering.
  • Fertilize Appropriately: Follow recommended schedules and quantities to avoid overfeeding your plants.
  • Control Pests Naturally: Use beneficial insects, neem oil, or organic sprays to keep pests at bay without harmful chemicals.
